Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fzyrk. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including our Resume Builder.

2. Data Collection & Privacy First

Our core philosophy is Privacy First. For our free tools (such as the free tier of our Resume Builder), we do not collect or store your resume data on our servers. All data you input remains stored locally in your browser's local storage. If you choose to export your resume to PDF, the rendering happens entirely in your browser using client-side libraries.

3. Information We Collect

If you subscribe to our Pro or Team plans, we collect information necessary to manage your account and billing, including:

  • Name and Email Address
  • Billing information (processed securely via our payment partners; we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Resume data (if you explicitly choose to sync your resumes to the cloud for multi-device access)

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and store your theme preferences. We also use basic analytics to understand how users interact with our website to improve our services, with IP addresses anonymized.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@fzyrk.com.
